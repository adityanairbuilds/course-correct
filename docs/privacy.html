Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Course Correct does not collect any personally identifiable information. No name, email address, phone number, or account is required to use the app.

What We Collect

An anonymous, randomly generated ID stored locally on your device to prevent duplicate ratings, and course ratings you voluntarily submit.

What We Don't Collect

Your name, email, or any contact information, your location, or any information that can identify you personally.

How Data Is Used

Submitted ratings are stored in aggregate and displayed to other users to help students make informed course decisions.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
